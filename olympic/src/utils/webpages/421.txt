Who we are

            

                Who we are

Athlete365 supports athletes on and off the field of play through relevant advice, services and tools. We encourage athletes to lead conversations and work across sport to deliver an athlete-led approach. Our activity is focused on key areas that address an Athlete’s needs no matter where they are on their journey. Our mission is to make Athletes’ lives better and to support the world’s biggest community of elite and Olympic level Athletes.

What we do

            

                What we do

Athletes are at the heart of the Olympic Movement. We encourage athletes to lead, shape and progress the conversations and activities that flow through the Athlete365 community. We focus on key areas that address Athletes’ needs no matter where you are on your journey. We provide Athletes with the platform to share your experiences, effect change and make decisions that shape the future of sport.

                            Music in Sport                        
                                                
                                

As an athlete using music in your routine, you need to respect the legal rights in the music used. This applies to any sports discipline where music is a core element, such as artistic and rhythmic gymnastics; figure skating, synchronized swimming and dressage. If you are a parent or guardian of a child athlete using music, please read this guidance carefully on your child’s behalf.

IOC Athletes' Commission Election
    
                    
                

The election will take place in the Athlete365 space at Beijing 2022 and all athletes participating at the Beijing 2022 Olympic Games will be eligible to vote.
            
                

    
        
            
                                        
                            

            
        
        
                            
                
                            
                
                            
                
                    
                    
                                
                                            
                            Meet the Candidates                        
                                                
                                

Meet the full list of candidates for the IOC AC Election that will be competing for your all-important votes during the Games in Beijing.
                            
                                            
            
                        
            
                        
                                

                    
    


    
                        
                    Can I vote?


All accredited athletes (AA) participating in the Olympic Games Beijing 2022 are eligible to vote.
                
                                
                    Where can I vote?


At the Olympic Villages; the voting booths are located in the Athlete365 Spaces.
