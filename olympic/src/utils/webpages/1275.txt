English[edit]
English Wikipedia has an article on:Nova ScotiaWikipedia 
Etymology[edit]


From Latin, literally “New Scotland”, from nova, feminine of novus (“new”) + Scōtia (“Scotland”).

Pronunciation[edit]
(Canada, General American) IPA(key): /ˌnoʊ.və ˈskoʊ.ʃə/
(Received Pronunciation) IPA(key): /ˌnəʊ.və ˈskəʊ.ʃə/
Proper noun[edit]


Nova Scotia

A province in eastern Canada. Capital: Halifax.
A peninsula on the coast of the Atlantic, comprising most of the province of Nova Scotia.
