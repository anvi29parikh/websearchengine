	Wikipedia:Ignore all rules
	
		From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

		Wikipedia page explaining the policy that Wikipedia does not have firm rules




This page documents an English Wikipedia policy.
It describes a widely accepted standard that all editors should normally follow. Changes made to it should reflect consensus.ShortcutsWP:IARWP:IGNOREw.wiki/i


If a rule prevents you from improving or maintaining Wikipedia, ignore it.
