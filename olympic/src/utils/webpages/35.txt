        IOC Refugee Olympic Team Tokyo 2020
        
            

Twenty-nine athletes competing across 12 sports and from 13 host National Olympic Committees (NOCs). The athletes were selected from among the refugee athletes currently supported by the IOC through the Olympic Scholarships for Refugee Athletes programme.&nbsp;
