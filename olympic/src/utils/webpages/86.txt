Welcome to the International Olympic Committee media database.


By creating your profile and signing in, you will be able to subscribe to receive IOC press releases and request accreditation to cover IOC institutional events.


With a unique username and password, you will be able to register your profile, update your data and apply for media accreditations.


Click on Media Events on the side menu if you want to see the events for which the period to apply for media accreditation is currently open.
