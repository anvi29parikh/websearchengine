Icon that typically represents the autopatrolled user right on MediaWiki


Users in autopatrol group can:

Have one's own edits automatically marked as patrolled (autopatrol)


Members of the administrators group already have the user right above.


The membership of this group can be given by administrators and bureaucrats to rarely but regularly active users. Many global groups such as stewards, global sysops, global rollbackers and Wikimedia staff members already have autopatrol, and do not need to request this right as a result.
