English[edit]
Proper noun[edit]


Wiki

(proscribed) Abbreviation of Wikipedia.
Wiki is where I go to research facts about Asian culture.
Usage notes[edit]
Some people criticize this usage because it may lead to confusion with the generic word wiki for a type of user-editable site. See the essay "Don't abbreviate "Wikipedia" as "Wiki"!".
