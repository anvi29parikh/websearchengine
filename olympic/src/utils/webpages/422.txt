Who we are

            

                Who we are

Athlete365 supports athletes on and off the field of play through relevant advice, services and tools. We encourage athletes to lead conversations and work across sport to deliver an athlete-led approach. Our activity is focused on key areas that address an Athlete’s needs no matter where they are on their journey. Our mission is to make Athletes’ lives better and to support the world’s biggest community of elite and Olympic level Athletes.

What we do

            

                What we do

Athletes are at the heart of the Olympic Movement. We encourage athletes to lead, shape and progress the conversations and activities that flow through the Athlete365 community. We focus on key areas that address Athletes’ needs no matter where you are on your journey. We provide Athletes with the platform to share your experiences, effect change and make decisions that shape the future of sport.

                About Us


Athlete365 has been created by athletes, for athletes, and our mission is to make Athletes’ lives better and to grow the world’s biggest community of elite and Olympic level Athletes. By providing relevant advice, services and tools Athlete365 is unique positioned to support Athletes in the areas that they need it the most and at every stage of their career. We promise to support athletes on their journey to the Olympic Games and beyond by providing digital and physical spaces where Athletes can learn, have their voice heard and feel connected to the wider athlete community.
            
                

    
        
            
                                        
                            

            
        
        
                            
                
                            
                
                            
                
                    
                    
                                
                                            
                            IOC Athletes’ Commission                        
                                                
                                

The IOC Athletes’ Commission serves as a link between the athletes and the IOC. The Mission of the IOC Athletes’ Commission is to ensure that the athletes’ viewpoint remains at the heart of the Olympic Movement decisions.

                The Olympic Movement


The goal of the Olympic Movement is to contribute to building a peaceful and better world through sport practised in accordance with Olympism and its values. Together we work towards achieving that goal.
            
                

    
        
            
                                        
                            

    
        
                            
                
                    
                    
                                    
                    
                                
                                            
                            Olympic.org                        
                                                
                                International Olympic Committee


What is it?

The IOC is the governing body of the Olympic Movement.


How does it help Athletes?

It ensures athletes’ interests are kept at the heart of the Olympic Movement.

What is it?

Olympic Channel&nbsp;is an&nbsp;over-the-top&nbsp;Internet television&nbsp;service operated by the&nbsp;IOC.


How does it help Athletes?

It engages fans by telling the stories of Athletes.

                            olympians.org                        
                                                
                                World Olympians Association


What is it?

An organisation which works for the benefit of all Olympians at all stages of their lives.


How does it help Athletes?

It creates a range of benefits for Olympians assisting them in transitioning to a life beyond competitive sport following retirement.

                            OLYMPIC.ORG/OLYMPIC-SOLIDARITY                        
                                                
                                Olympic Solidarity


What is it?

An IOC programme which organises assistance for all National Olympic Committees through multi-faceted programmes.


How does it help Athletes?

Ensures that the revenue generated during the Olympic Games is redistributed to those Athletes who need it the most.

                                International Federations


Who are they?

The International Sports Federations (IFs) are 40 international non-governmental organisations recognised by the International Olympic Committee.


How does it help Athletes?

International Federations supervise the development of athletes practising their sports and provide and govern their sports global competition structure.
                            
                                                        
                                
                                    Visit site                                                                    
                            
                                            
            
                        
            
                        
                                            
                            

    
        
                            
                
                    
                    
                                    
                    
                                
                                                
                                National Olympic Committees&nbsp;


Who are they?

There are 206 National Olympic Committees (NOCs) who, together with the IFs, are a constituent of the Olympic Movement under the leadership of the IOC.


How does it help Athletes?

In accordance with the Olympic Charter, NOCs develop, promote and protect the values of the Olympic Movement within their respective countries and are responsible for selecting and supporting athletes for the Olympic Games.
