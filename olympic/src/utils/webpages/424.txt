Who we are

            

                Who we are

Athlete365 supports athletes on and off the field of play through relevant advice, services and tools. We encourage athletes to lead conversations and work across sport to deliver an athlete-led approach. Our activity is focused on key areas that address an Athlete’s needs no matter where they are on their journey. Our mission is to make Athletes’ lives better and to support the world’s biggest community of elite and Olympic level Athletes.

What we do

            

                What we do

Athletes are at the heart of the Olympic Movement. We encourage athletes to lead, shape and progress the conversations and activities that flow through the Athlete365 community. We focus on key areas that address Athletes’ needs no matter where you are on your journey. We provide Athletes with the platform to share your experiences, effect change and make decisions that shape the future of sport.

                About


The Athletes’ Entourage Commission advises the IOC Session, the IOC Executive Board and the IOC President on supporting and protecting clean athletes. The Commission’s aim is to improve the quality and the level of athlete services by engaging with and uniting key stakeholders.. The IOC AEC focuses its work on the relationship between athletes, parents, coaches, managers, technical officials, sponsors, media and all other stakeholders that support and work directly with athletes.

                            IOC Athletes' Entourage Commission 4-Year Plan                        
                                                
                                

As the important role of the athletes’ entourage becomes more exposed and significant within the Olympic Movement, an effective IOC Athletes’ Entourage Commission is of upmost importance. It is therefore essential for the Commission to have a clear focus and direction for the coming years to ensure their role is fulfilled and their work has maximum impact.

IOC Athletes' Entourage Commission members
    
                    
                

The IOC Athletes’ Entourage Commission is here to help you! To focus the Commission’s support across key areas, the IOC AEC divides its members into working groups on the following five themes:&nbsp;performance, health, family and friends, legal &amp; commercial and organisational. To find out more about these groups visit our entourage page.
