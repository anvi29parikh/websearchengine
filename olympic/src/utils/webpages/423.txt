Who we are

            

                Who we are

Athlete365 supports athletes on and off the field of play through relevant advice, services and tools. We encourage athletes to lead conversations and work across sport to deliver an athlete-led approach. Our activity is focused on key areas that address an Athlete’s needs no matter where they are on their journey. Our mission is to make Athletes’ lives better and to support the world’s biggest community of elite and Olympic level Athletes.

What we do

            

                What we do

Athletes are at the heart of the Olympic Movement. We encourage athletes to lead, shape and progress the conversations and activities that flow through the Athlete365 community. We focus on key areas that address Athletes’ needs no matter where you are on your journey. We provide Athletes with the platform to share your experiences, effect change and make decisions that shape the future of sport.

                About


The IOC Athletes’ Commission is an elected body which serves as a link between the athletes and the IOC. The Mission of the IOC Athletes’ Commission is to ensure that the athletes’ viewpoint remains at the heart of the Olympic Movement decisions.

                            IOC Athletes' Commission Strategy                         
                                                
                                

As elected athlete representatives in the Olympic Movement, we aim to lead by example and provide a guiding reference for other ACs. To do this successfully, we must be All In.


Launched in 2017, our Strategy has been a collective effort, which involved AC members from Continental Associations, International Federations and many other stakeholders within the Olympic Movement.

                            Athlete365 community app                        
                                                
                                

What is it?

The Athlete365 Community is the one place where AC members, Olympians and elite level athletes from across the Olympic movement can connect, share ideas and keep in touch.


How does it help Athletes?

The app helps to strengthen the athlete community by allowing you to create and run you own private or public groups, share expertise, experiences and key documents and stay up to date with regular updates from the Athlete365 community.
